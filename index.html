Improve your English listening and writing skills

How to Play

  1. Click the 🔊 Read Aloud button to hear a sentence
  2. Type what you hear in the input box
  3. Click "Check Answer" to see if you're correct
  4. Complete 5 correct answers to earn a Pokémon reward!
  5. You can click "Get New Sentence" for a different challenge
  6. Use "Show Answer" if you need help

Choose from different sentence banks or create your own custom bank in Settings.

The sentences are used to practice English listening and writing.
